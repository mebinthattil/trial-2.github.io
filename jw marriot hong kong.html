JW Marriot





Hotel details

Offering mountain views, this upmarket high-rise hotel is 7 minutes' walk from the nearest MTR station and 2 km from Victoria Harbour.
Warm rooms feature free Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs and minibars, plus mountain, harbour or city views. Upgraded rooms add access to a private lounge with complimentary breakfast. Suites have living areas and/or whirlpool baths. Room service is offered 24/7.
Dining options consist of Chinese, seafood and buffet restaurants, along with multiple bars. There's also an outdoor pool, a gym and a sauna, as well as a seasonal kids' club. Event space includes a ballroom and 16 meeting rooms. Parking is available (fee).
