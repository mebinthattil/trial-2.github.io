The Upper House





Hotel details

Set in a modern high-rise building, this chic hotel is a 6-minute walk from Admiralty metro station and 1 km from the Peak Tram to the Victoria Peak lookout point.
Refined, airy suites offer living areas, flat-screen TVs, and tea and coffeemaking facilities, as well as free minibars and Wi-Fi. They also have limestone bathtubs and rainfall showers. Upgraded suites add free-standing tubs and skyline views, while some include separate living rooms. The penthouse suites feature extra bedrooms.
Freebies include bike rentals and regular yoga classes. Amenities include a posh restaurant, a trendy cafe and a hip terrace bar, plus a gym.
