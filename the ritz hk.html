Hotel details




Set on floors 102 to 118 of the International Commerce Centre, this luxury hotel is 2 km from the Avenue of Stars waterfront promenade and 31 km from Hong Kong International Airport.
Sophisticated rooms and suites feature free Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs and iPhone docks, as well as Nespresso machines, minibars, and views of Victoria Harbour and/or the city skyline. Club level quarters provide complimentary snacks and drinks. Suites add living rooms, butler service and whirlpool tubs.
There are 3 refined restaurants, 2 bars (1 on the top floor) and a caviar bar. Other amenities include a spa with a gym and an indoor pool, plus a lavish ballroom.
