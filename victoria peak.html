Victoria Peak



Victoria Peak is a mountain in the western half of Hong Kong Island. It is also known as Mount Austin, and locally as The Peak.


Elevation: 554 m
Prominence: 554 m

Did you know:
Victoria Peak is the highest peak on Hong Kong Island with an altitude of 552 meters (1,811 feet) and is a landmark of the island.
